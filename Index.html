📋 RQFDS System Overview

The Survey Panelgo Respondent Quality & Fraud Detection System (RQFDS v3.2) is a proprietary multi-layer quality assurance engine deployed on all Survey Panelgo sample delivery projects. It operates in real-time, performing simultaneous checks across four dimensions: IP intelligence, device fingerprinting, behavioural biometrics, and duplicate detection.

The system runs 14 independent quality checks on every respondent before they can submit data. Responses that fail any CRITICAL check are automatically excluded from the dataset and replaced at no additional cost to the client.

All quality flags, scores, and removal reasons are logged to the project audit trail and delivered to clients as part of the standard QC Report included with every data delivery.

This system is aligned with the ESOMAR 37 Questions recommendations for online sample quality and meets the quality assurance requirements of the Survey Panelgo Panel Quality Standard (SP-QPS 2024).
